# THE DEEP NEURON · III/III

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE**.

THE DEEP NEURON — perceptron theory III of III, the BEGINNING one. THE TWIST: for 60 years a neuron = a perceptron (sum, threshold, fire). Then Beniaguev, Segev & London (2021, Neuron) trained a deep net to mimic a SINGLE simulated cortical neuron spike-for-spike — it took 5-8 LAYERS, because the dendrites (long treated as passive wires) do nonlinear computation (NMDA spikes). So one neuron may be a small deep net, and the perceptron is just one dendritic twig. LIVE: a point neuron (one perceptron, parks ~56% on an XOR-like task) vs a dendritic neuron (each branch a nonlinear ƒ = a hidden layer → 100%) — the gap verified in node. VERDICT = EARLY/FRONTIER: the finding is real & peer-reviewed, but '5-8' is one cell type at one fidelity (a characterization, not a constant), the demo ILLUSTRATES the principle (it does not simulate a biophysical neuron), and whether AI should adopt dendritic units is wide open.
